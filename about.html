About Redline Racing Alliance

Redline Racing Alliance was created by a group of sim racers who wanted structured NASCAR ’25 league racing without the toxic drama that sometimes comes with it. Our goal is to combine serious on-track competition with a relaxed, respectful community off the track.

We race exclusively on PC using NASCAR ’25. Our events emphasize clean driving, awareness and communication. Mistakes happen – intent and attitude matter most.

What We Offer

  • Organized series with published schedules and clear rules.
  • Multiple divisions to fit different skill and commitment levels.
  • Voice chat on Discord for race control and socializing.
  • Points standings and (optional) team championships.

What We Expect

  • Respect for other drivers and admins.
  • Clean, fair racing – no intentional wrecking.
  • Reasonable attendance if you commit to a season.
  • Willingness to learn, improve and have fun.
